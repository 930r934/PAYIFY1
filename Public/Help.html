HELP

Home:
This will redirect you to Home page, In this you will find three Functions that you can perform
1 Profile management: In this you can update your personal information
2 wallet management: In this you can check your wallet amount and update your bank details
3 Shopping Cart: In this you can see the items that you have added to buy and then you can proceed to checkout

Shop:
This contain two functionalities that you can perform
1 phones: In this you can view the mobiles that are available and you can add to Your cart
2 accessories: In this you can view the accessories and can add to cart if you wish to buy

Book:
This contain two functionalities that you can perform
1 movies: In this you can book tickets for any movie that you want to and can pay money with any payment method present in wallet management
2 bus: In this you can book tickets for sourse to destination and can select the payment method from the wallet management

Bill:
This Contain two functionalities that you can perform
1 Electric City: In this you can select the operator and pay your electric-city bill using any payment method avaliable in wallet-Management
2 Mobile Recharge: In this Module you can recharge your mobile by selecting the operator and paying through any method
